ABOUT US

Small Shovel's core focus is to offer specialized control system and information technology resources to electric utilities, enabling them to create, implement, and sustain operational technology solutions while supporting and guiding their staff. The company's founders - David Shpigler, Tim Weidenbach, and Glenn Purcell - bring in their expertise to focus on areas such as AMI/MDM, GIS, CVR, and OMS support. With its proven support framework, Small Shovel empowers utilities to optimize their operational technology investments and enhance their total cost of ownership.
